Mission: Brief History of Company

Omo'n'Efe Designs started off with a collection of bags and has expand its product portfolio successively to eventually become a well-known brand for Fashion accessories and garment for fashion-conscious women



Vission

Omo’n’Efe was founded with a vision to empower women and men in Nigeria to express themselves freelythrough fashion. The brand pushes the boundaries of modern fashion and accessories by constantly reinventing fashion with its curated designs.Since its establishment in 2013, the brand has expanded its reach beyond sewing and have expand its product portfolio successively to accessory making and training .

Aim and Philosophy

Our philosophy is to Inspire the world through Fashion, and create a better future for aspire fashion entrepreneurs.The philosophy is to design an end to end successful process that is customer focus in every aspect of our business. We value excellence as our main principle and we aim to provide quality training to our student.We train interested individuals to become fashion entrepreneurs with a unique approach of blending western fashion styles with our indigenous African fabrics and styles. We offer 4 weeks to 12 months classroom training in Short Courses, Beginners to Professional/ Specialized Sewing and Pattern Construction/Designing and still expanding.

Object - Business Activity

OMO’n’EFE is a productive fashion and training fashion house with focus in trendy and sophisticated Fashion Design, Fashion Consultancy and Fashion Training. We offer a fast-paced curriculum in garment making, fashion accessories design. We specialize in making dazzling handmade African pieces like bags,bangles, earrings, neckpiece, hair accessories, clutch bag Slippers, amongst others. We serve cooperate bodies and individuals.
